WATCH: ZEROBASEONE makes comeback with bright 'Feel the Pop' MV

 
From ABS-CBN News
Published May 13, 2024 05:54 PM PHT | Updated May 13, 2024 06:32 PM PHT
 
 
K-pop group ZEROBASEONE in a scene from the music video of its single 'Feel the Pop.' Screenshot from video on ZEROBASEONE's YouTube channel
 
Rising K-pop boy group ZEROBASEONE made a comeback on Monday with its third extended play (EP), accompanied by the music video for the laidback single "Feel the Pop."

The nine-member band, formed through the 2023 reality talent competition show "Boys Planet," dropped the seven-track EP "You Had Me At Hello," with "Feel the Pop" serving as its main track.

The "Feel the Pop" video mostly features the ZEROBASEONE members donning cowboy attire against a cityscape backdrop, with large balloon-like objects bursting in the heavily-lit sky, illuminated by a big, bright sun.

In other scenes, the boys are seen dancing on a water-filled tennis court, emanating a refreshing summer vibe akin to the video for the EP's pre-release track "Sweat."

Apart from "Feel the Pop" and "Sweat," the EP also includes "Solar Power," "Dear Eclipse," "Sunday Ride" and "Hello" as well as a sped-up version of the lead single.

ZEROBASEONE, with over a million X (formerly Twitter) followers, earlier announced it would embark on its "The First Tour" beginning September.

The tour includes a stop at the Mall of Asia Arena on October 12, marking ZEROBASEONE's return to the country since attending the Asia Artist Awards at the Philippine Arena last December.
